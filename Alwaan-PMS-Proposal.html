What it does

Capabilities That Replace 5 Apps

The system covers every step of the property lifecycle — from the day a tenant signs to the day they move out — in one unified tool.

🆔

Emirates ID OCR

Upload the EID photo — name, nationality, ID number, DOB, expiry, visa and passport data auto-filled.

📄

Ejari PDF Import

Drag in the DLD Ejari PDF and the system extracts property, owner, tenant, and contract terms in seconds.

💳

Cheque Lifecycle

Track every PDC from Received → Deposited → Cleared or Bounced. Overdue alerts and due-today filters.

🏦

Bank Reconciliation

Upload your bank statement — the system auto-matches credits to cheques by number, amount, and date.

📊

Real-Time Dashboards

Occupancy, rent roll, cashflow, P&L — updated every 60 seconds without lifting a finger.

📱

Multi-Portal Access

Staff manage, owners monitor, tenants self-serve. Each role sees only what they need.

📤

Bulk Imports

Upload Excel with 236 tenants and 1,329 cheques in one go — with preview and validation.

🔒

Secure by Design

Role-based access, encrypted passwords, signed magic links, httpOnly session cookies.

📥

CSV + PDF Export

Every table, every page exports. Print any dashboard as a client-ready PDF.
